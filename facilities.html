The CRES²T Mission

A collaborative research incubator...

Penn State
🔭 PSUDO Observatory

0.6m RC Telescope in Animus, NM. Tracks LEO/GEO objects including Starlink, COSMOS rocket bodies, and GEO satellites.

🤖 GRIP Lab (DURIP 2026)

Gravity-Offloaded Robotics & Proximity Operations. Supports partial gravity testing, docking, rendezvous, in-space repair, and astronaut training.

🥽 Cislunar VR Lab

Online orbit & manifold generation, Poincaré maps, uncertainty propagation, and rendezvous/proximity operations with coupled orbit-attitude dynamics.

Purdue
🌕 Cislunar Initiative

Since 2019, leverages strengths in mission design and propulsion. Focus areas: infrastructure, space resources, frequent access, education, and space policy/economics/defense.

🧪 LASR Laboratory

Land Air & Space Robotics — Three 6-DOF motion emulation platforms, 60×90 ft area for proximity operation experiments, ground truth sensing systems.

⚙️ RAD Laboratory

Robotics & Automation Design — 8-DOF motion emulation platform and UR-20 robotic arms for space assembly and manufacturing.

Texas A&M
🏛️ Texas Space Commission

Space industry research funding and student programs. CRES²T is on TSC's list of synergistic partners.

🌍 A&M Space Institute

Mars and Moonscapes for surface robotics testing, industry technology transition spaces.

✈️ SPIRIT Program

Flight testing for space technologies. Partnership with AEGIS Aerospace.
