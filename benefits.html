Benefits of Membership

CRES²T research provides direct value to members through a member-driven research agenda. The IAB votes on proposed projects that are scientifically meritorious and aligned with industry interests.
💰

Funding Leverage

  • Leveraged investment via pooled member resources
  • NSF-mandated 10% overhead vs. up to 65% for sponsored research
  • Additional grant opportunities via NSF IUCRC network
🎓

Access to Talent

  • Graduate students and early-career researchers for recruitment
  • World-class engineers and long-term faculty relationships
  • Network with industry peers, players, and customers
🔬

Access to Technology

  • Industry-driven precompetitive research
  • Access to results pre-publication
  • Early IP licensing (royalty-free or exclusive)
