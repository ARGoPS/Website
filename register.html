By Air

The local airport in State College is the State College Airport (SCE). It is located approximately five miles from campus.

Taxis and car rentals are available at University Park Airport to get from the airport to your final destination in State College.

By Train

State College does not have a train station. The closest train station is 45 minutes away in Lewistown, PA. There is no other public transportation from Lewistown to State College.

The closest train station with a bus connection to State College is in Harrisburg, PA.

By Car

State College is conveniently located near several major cities and airports. The campus is easily accessible via Interstate 99 and US Route 322.

Approximate drive times from nearby cities:

  • Harrisburg: 1.5 hours
  • Pittsburgh: 3 hours
  • Philadelphia: 3.5 hours
  • Baltimore: 3 hours
  • Washington DC: 4 hours
  • New York City: 5 hours
